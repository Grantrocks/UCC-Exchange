We provide completely a anonymous exchange for our currency Uncracked Coin. The API details are below.

All data should be sent as JSON.

We take a 1% fee on all sell orders.

List of supported buy/sell currencys

  1. USDT - Tether USD
  2. SOL - Solana
  3. XNO - NANO
  • /buy - Create a buy order.
    FORMAT: {"address":"provide your ucc address","currency":"one of our suppoerted currencys","amount":amount to purchase (integer)","dex":True or False (boolean)}
  • /sell - Sell your UCC for another currency.
    FORMAT: {"address":"address of the currency you are exhanging ucc for","currency":"one of our supported currencys","payment_address":"address used to send UCC","txid":"transaction id from the UCC blockchain.", "dex":"True or False"}
  • /stats - View information on the UCC currency.

We also support DEX or decentrealized. However that is usually a slower process as your buy/sell orders have to be matched up. Although decentrealized is fee free.
